Electric App

Instructions:

This programs introduces an easy and intuitive way to visualize and understand the effects of different charges on their surroundings. Simply put the value of any charge, it's coordinates (based on any X-Y coordinate system, the program will adjust) and click whether you would like to calculate the surrounding electric potential, fields or forces the charges have on each other; note that electric potential will take a moment to load. In addition, you can click anywhere on the image and below will be shown the electric potential and field magnitude at that point. If you are unfamiliar with what would be a good charge value or a normal distance or just want to see a cool image, click "Auto-Generate" to have a set of randomly selected appropriate values inputed. Enjoy!
